Privacy Policy

Brief does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app retrieves aviation weather data (METAR and TAF) from publicly available sources. No personal data is sent to or stored by Brief.

If analytics or crash reporting tools are used, they may collect anonymous usage data to help improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Brief does not use tracking for advertising purposes.
