Privacy Policy

BriefWX is built to keep aviation weather lookup simple without collecting personal information.

No Personal Data Collection

BriefWX does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Weather Data

The app retrieves aviation weather data, including METAR and TAF information, from publicly available sources. No personal data is sent to or stored by BriefWX.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

If analytics or crash reporting tools are used, they may collect anonymous usage data to help improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Advertising

BriefWX does not use tracking for advertising purposes.
